Taiwan traveling list just for you

Taiwan is devided into basically 4+1 regions: In the main land of Taiwan, there are Northern Taiwan, Central Taiwan, Southern Taiwan and Eastern Taiwan. Outlying Island includes Green island, Penghu, Lambai Island etc. In this page, you will see the recommeded tourism spots from the North to South, The East and outlying islands.

Dom: Dom: where I currently at. The weatehr is nice. there are spots like Sun and moon lake, a nice lake that you can drive/cycle/e-bike around, mountains and temples surround the lake. Nice sunrise and sunset views. I’ve been twice so unless you really like the look of it I’m happy to miss it lol. The South spots

Dom: Kaohsiung is nicer than Taipei IMO, right on the beach (black sand beach), a few nice short hikes around the city. Very cheap, can get nice hotels for a bargain. Closest city to Kenting which is the nicest beach area of the main Taiwan island so could be easy to spend a night or two here to break up the journey to Kenting. Also has Fo Guang Shan. Also close to Tainan, ancient capital of Taiwan, famous for food and old buildings. Quite underdeveloped, only recently got serviced by uber. No real public transport (you can rent a scooter for NT$400/day).
